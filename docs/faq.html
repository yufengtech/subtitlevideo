🎬 基本使用 🎬 Basic Usage

如何导入视频文件？ How to import video files? ▼
您可以通过以下方式导入视频：
1. 点击「选择视频」按钮，从相册中选择
2. 使用文件应用共享视频到即幕视频
3. 从其他应用分享视频到即幕视频
支持的格式包括：MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WMV, FLV等 You can import videos in the following ways:
1. Tap "Select Video" button to choose from photo library
2. Share videos from Files app to SubtitleVideo
3. Share videos from other apps to SubtitleVideo
Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WMV, FLV, etc.
如何开启字幕功能？ How to enable subtitle feature? ▼
1. 播放视频后，点击屏幕显示控制界面
2. 点击「字幕」按钮开启实时字幕生成
3. 应用会自动识别视频中的语音并生成字幕
4. 首次使用需要授权麦克风权限 1. After playing video, tap screen to show controls
2. Tap "Subtitle" button to enable real-time subtitle generation
3. App will automatically recognize speech and generate subtitles
4. Microphone permission required for first use

⚙️ 设置与自定义 ⚙️ Settings & Customization

如何调整字幕大小？ How to adjust subtitle size? ▼
1. 进入设置页面
2. 选择「字幕大小」选项
3. 从小、中、大、特大中选择合适的大小
4. 设置会立即生效，无需重启应用 1. Go to Settings page
2. Select "Subtitle Size" option
3. Choose from Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large
4. Changes take effect immediately

🔧 故障排除 🔧 Troubleshooting

字幕识别不准确怎么办？ What if subtitle recognition is inaccurate? ▼
可以尝试以下解决方法：
1. 确保视频音质清晰，背景噪音较少
2. 在设置中选择正确的识别语言
3. 确保设备有良好的网络连接
4. 重启应用再次尝试
5. 如果问题持续，请联系技术支持 Try these solutions:
1. Ensure video has clear audio with minimal background noise
2. Select correct recognition language in settings
3. Ensure device has good network connection
4. Restart app and try again
5. Contact support if issue persists
应用崩溃或卡顿怎么办？ What to do if app crashes or freezes? ▼
请按以下步骤排查：
1. 强制关闭应用并重新打开
2. 重启设备
3. 确保iOS系统版本为15.0或更高
4. 释放设备存储空间
5. 更新应用到最新版本
6. 如问题仍存在，请发送崩溃日志给我们 Follow these troubleshooting steps:
1. Force close app and reopen
2. Restart device
3. Ensure iOS version is 15.0 or higher
4. Free up device storage space
5. Update app to latest version
6. Send crash logs if problem persists

🔒 隐私与权限 🔒 Privacy & Permissions

为什么需要麦克风权限？ Why does the app need microphone permission? ▼
麦克风权限用于：
• 生成实时字幕
• 我们不会录制或保存您的音频数据
• 您可以随时在设置中关闭此权限 Microphone permission is used for:
• Extracting audio from videos for speech recognition
• Generating real-time subtitles
• We don't record or save your audio data
• All speech recognition is done locally on device
• You can disable this permission anytime in settings
我的视频数据安全吗？ Is my video data secure? ▼
是的，您的数据完全安全：
• 所有视频处理均在设备本地进行
• 我们不会上传或存储您的视频文件
• 语音识别使用iOS原生API，在设备上完成
• 应用遵循最严格的隐私保护标准
• 详情请查看我们的隐私政策 Yes, your data is completely secure:
• All video processing is done locally on device
• We don't upload or store your video files
• Speech recognition uses native iOS APIs on device
• App follows strictest privacy protection standards
• See our privacy policy for details
